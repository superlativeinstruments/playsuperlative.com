a design studio with a vision of creating the best in electronic musical instruments
The sound of the past
crafted for the future
Picking up where the golden age of analog synthesizer design left off, Superlative is building an ecosystem of instruments for the next generation of musicians. We believe that the best in electronic instruments is yet to come.

As musicians as well as engineers, we know that players are empowered by having access to portable, durable, and professional instruments they can depend on.

We want to design products that last - both in their original design and with the goal that their repair and maintenance be as transparent as possible.

Our synthesizers are engineered and built in the U.S.
